5. ATTENDANCE RULE ENGINE (VERY IMPORTANT)

Attendance behavior must be rule-driven, not random.

Rule Examples:

Late if after 09:00 AM

Absent if no check-in by 10:00 AM

Half-day if checkout before 1:00 PM

Friday / holiday auto-excluded

Exam days follow different rules

Rules must be:

Class-wise

Shift-wise

Institution-wise
